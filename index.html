About VRS

Roadside emergencies, solved instantly.

VRS connects stranded vehicle owners with verified nearby mechanics in seconds. Whether it's a flat tire at midnight or an engine breakdown on the highway, help is just a tap away.

Our platform uses real-time GPS tracking, AI-powered diagnostics, and a trust-based verification system to ensure fast, reliable, and safe roadside assistance.
